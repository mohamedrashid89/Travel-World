Doha Tower

The Skyscraper Center

The building is designed to hold offices for businesses wishing to operate in the diverse business district of West Bay. The Doha Tower is owned by Sheikh Saud bin Muhammed Al Thani and managed by Hamad Bin Saoud Group. It is currently the sixth tallest building in Doha.

Egyptian pyramid

The Great Pyramid of Giza

The Great Pyramid of Giza is the largest Egyptian pyramid and the tomb of Fourth Dynasty pharaoh Khufu. Built in the early 26th century BCE during a period of around 27 years, the pyramid is the oldest of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World, and the only one to remain largely intact.

Mie Prefecture

Mie Prefecture

Mie Prefecture is a popular tourism destination home to Nagashima Spa Land, Suzuka International Racing Course, and some of the oldest and holiest sites in Shinto, the traditional religion of Japan, including the Ise Grand Shrine and the Tsubaki Grand Shrine.

Big Ben

Palace of Westminster

Big Ben is the nickname for the Great Bell of the Great Clock of Westminster, at the north end of the Palace of Westminster in London, England, and the name is frequently extended to refer also to the clock and the clock tower.

Arabian Desert

Saudi Arabian Desert

Including the world's largest and driest sand desert—the 'Rub'al-Khali'—the region lies predominantly in Saudi Arabia, crossing into Yemen and Oman on its southern fringes, and the United Arab Emirates in the east. Although 'Rub'al-Khali' translates from Arabic as 'The Empty Quarter', this area is far from desolate.

Gardens by the Bay

Gardens by the Bay Singapore

The Gardens by the Bay is a nature park spanning 101 hectares in the Central Region of Singapore, adjacent to the Marina Reservoir. The park consists of three waterfront gardens: Bay South Garden, Bay East Garden and Bay Central Garden.
